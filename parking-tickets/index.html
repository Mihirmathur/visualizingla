LA Parking Tickets

Parking tickets are something that everyone has to deal with in Los Angeles. We have analyzed almost 3000 tickets from the past 3 years and projected them onto a map of Los Angeles.

Our best tip if you want to avoid a ticket, stay away from downtown Los Angeles or any of the many touristy spots around Los Angeles.

The map data gets more and more precise the more that you zoom in, down to the very steet and cost of the fine. To get more ticket info just click or mouse over the hexagons.

Technologies used: Leaflet, Mapbox, D3, JQuery Data sources: Parking Citations
